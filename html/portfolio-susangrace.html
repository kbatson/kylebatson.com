Susan Grace Artist's Website

www.susangracestudio.com

I designed and built this website for the talented painter, Susan Grace. It was built on top of an ExpressionEngine back-end giving the artist the ability to easily update her own portfolio.

I also provided branding and designed print collateral for use in her shows, as well as shot photographs to archive her work.

Skills Utilized:

  • Website Design
  • ExpressionEngine Development
  • Branding/Identity
  • Front-end Development (CSS, HTML, Javascript)
  • Photography
