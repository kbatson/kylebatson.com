User Experience Strategy

As the lead for a newly formed User Experience team, I guided the team and company through the creation of process for product design that introduced UX and UI design at the beginning of the product development cycle. This involved bringing in more research to better understand users and their tasks. The team documented their research as user Personas and created Journey Maps to help create better understanding and empathy towards the users throughout the product team.

Skills Utilized:

  • User Experience Design
  • Team management
  • Systems analysis
  • Documentation
